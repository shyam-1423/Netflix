1. Cruelty 24m

After selling charcoal in town, Tanjiro returns home to find his whole family dead. Only his sister Nezuko has survived -- but she’s changed.

2. Trainer Sakonji Urokodaki 24m

Tanjiro departs for the mountains with Nezuko in tow. En route, he smells blood coming from inside a temple, where he stumbles upon a gruesome scene.

3. Sabito and Makomo 24m

Under Sakonji, Tanjiro trains to become a member of the Demon Slayer Corps. But before he can join, he'll have to qualify for the Final Selection.

4. Final Selection 24m

The Final Selection begins. Tanjiro and the other trainees must survive for seven days among demons trapped alive by Demon Slayer swordsmen.

5. My Own Steel 24m

Tanjiro is among the four survivors of the Final Selection. After acquiring a new sword, he receives orders for his first mission as a Demon Slayer.

6. Swordsman Accompanying a Demon 24m

For his first mission, Tanjiro heads northwest to a village where he meets Kazumi, whose lover has disappeared without a trace.

7. Muzan Kibutsuji 24m

Tanjiro dives into a swamp realm to confront a swamp demon who's split into three, but the demon refuses to tell him anything about Muzan Kibutsuji.

8. The Smell of Enchanting Blood 24m

For his next mission, Tanjiro heads to the bustling area of Asakusa, Tokyo to find the demon hiding among the crowds.

9. Temari Demon and Arrow Demon 24m

While taking refuge inside a home concealed by Yushiro's spell, Tanjiro protects those inside from demons sent by Muzan Kibutsuji.

10. Together Forever 24m

Nezuko goes toe-to-toe with Susamaru and her deadly volley of temari balls, but Tamayo intervenes with her own spell before the battle intensifies.

11. Tsuzumi Mansion 24m

Tanjiro travels south-southeast for his next mission. On the way, he encounters fellow swordsman and Final Selection survivor, Zenitsu Agatsuma.

12. The Boar Bares Its Fangs, Zenitsu Sleeps 24m

As Zenitsu cowers in fear inside a mysterious rotating house, Tanjiro faces a drum-beating demon and an odd sword-wielding man wearing a boar mask.

13. Something More Important Than Life 24m

With the house turning with each beat of the drum, an injured Tanjiro struggles against Kyogai’s overwhelming attack.

14. The House with the Wisteria Family Crest 24m

Now outside the cursed drum house, Tanjiro finds a beaten and bloody Zenitsu, and is confronted by the belligerent man wearing a boar's head mask.

15. Mount Natagumo 24m

Tanjiro travels north-northeast with Inosuke and Zenitsu on their next mission. They reach Natagumo Mountain, where a spider horde resides.

16. Letting Someone Else Go First 24m

With their fellow Demon Slayers under the demon spider's control, Tanjiro and Inosuke must figure a way to subdue their comrades without harming them.

17. You Must Master a Single Thing 24m

After facing the mother spider, Tanjiro learns of a demon under Muzan Kibutsuji's direct command who may hold the key to turning Nezuko back to human.

18. A Forged Bond 24m

While taking on the enraged father spider demon with Inosuke, Tanjiro is flung far away, where he confronts the spider demon, Rui.

19. Hinokami 24m

Giyu Tomioka, a top-ranked swordsman of the Demon Killing Corps, arrives. Outclassed and in awe of his skills, Inosuke challenges him to a duel.

20. Pretend Family 24m

Tanjiro prepares for the worst when faced with Rui’s spell. But then, the memory of his father dancing the Kagura inspires him to unleash a new move.

21. Against Corps Rules 24m

Seeing a heavily injured Tanjiro still try to defend Nezuko, Rui is reminded of the one thing he craves and doesn’t have: true family connection.

22. Master of the Mansion 24m

Tanjiro is dragged before a gathering of the Hashira, the most powerful of Demon Slayer Corps swordsmen, to face judgment.

23. Hashira Meeting 24m

Despite Ubuyashiki's decision to accept Tanjiro and Nezuko into the Corps, Sanemi tries to draw out Nezuko's demonic bloodthirst.

24. Rehabilitation Training 24m

Tanjiro and other Corps members receive treatment at Shinobu's estate. Two weeks later, he and Inosuke begin training to rebuild their strength.

25. Tsuguko, Kanao Tsuyuri 24m

Tanjiro trains hard to maintain Total Concentration Breathing all day long. Fearing getting left behind, Zenitsu and Inosuke start training again too.
